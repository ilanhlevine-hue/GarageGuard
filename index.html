5th Grader · Entrepreneur · Baseball Player

Hi! I'm Ilan, and I created Garage Guard! 👋 I'm a 5th grader at Sunrise Park and a proud resident of Lotus Palm. I play travel baseball for the West Boca Panthers ⚾ and I've always loved coming up with new ideas and ways to help people.

I noticed that people in our neighborhood didn't have anyone to watch their packages when they went on vacation — so I decided to fix that! 💡 I built Garage Guard because I wanted to do something that actually helps our community AND learn what it's like to run a real business.
